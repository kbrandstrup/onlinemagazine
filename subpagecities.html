A Stroll Through Edinburgh
by Kristy Brandstrup

Edinburgh is the capital of Scotland. With a unique hilly landscape, it has a medieval Old Town and elegant Georgian New Town. Edinburgh Castle looms over the city, which is home to Scotland’s crown jewels and the Stone of Destiny.

As you walk the streets of Edinburgh, you will be in awe of all its beauty. It has a charm quite unlike any other city, with both nature and gardens surrounding mixed with old historical buildings. You can feel the pride of the Scottish people as they play their bagpipes on the street.

There is much to explore in the city of Edinburgh. The shopping streets are always a fun day activity as you get to walk through and experience the different boutiques - both old and new. You will find that tweed and kilt stores are popular among the doorways as the fabric used for these fine articles of clothing are made from the wool of their beloved hardy local sheep.

Apart from the Edinburgh castle being the most eye- catching part of the city, there is also Arthur’s Seat. This iconic hill is an ancient volcano, which is the main peak of the group of hills in Edinburgh, sitting in Holyrood Park. After spending a day shopping and eating, you can take a nice little hike up Arthur’s Seat and get a fantastic view of the city. Along the way you will also find some ruins, as you will see throughout the entirety of Scotland as well.

Edinburgh is the perfect city to visit for both its historic charm and its extraordinary landscape. There is much to explore in this beautiful city.
